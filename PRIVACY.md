# Download Novel Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Download Novel processes webpage content locally in the user's browser to create EPUB files for offline reading.

## Data the extension accesses

- The URL, title, and HTML content of a webpage when the user opens the extension or starts a download.
- Chapter pages and images needed to build the requested EPUB.
- Local download progress and preferences stored through Chrome's extension storage.

## How data is used

The extension uses this data only to identify book metadata, locate chapters, remove non-book page elements, build an EPUB, and show download progress.

## Data sharing and retention

Download Novel does not sell, transmit, or share user data with the developer or third parties. Processing occurs locally in Chrome. The extension does not use analytics, advertising, tracking, or a developer-operated server.

The extension stores limited job status and preferences locally in Chrome. Users can remove this data by uninstalling the extension or clearing its extension data.

Requests to novel websites are made directly from the user's browser to the website selected by the user. Those websites may apply their own privacy policies and terms.

## Limited Use

Download Novel's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs complies with the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. User data is used only to provide the extension's single purpose, is not sold or used for advertising or credit decisions, and is not made available for humans to read.

## Permissions

- `activeTab` and `scripting`: read the page selected by the user.
- Host access: fetch chapter pages and images from the novel website selected by the user.
- `downloads`: save the generated EPUB and report its download status.
- `storage`: retain local progress and preferences.
