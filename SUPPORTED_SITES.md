# Supported Sites

Download Novel now supports site-specific parsers for:

- WTR-LAB
- RoyalRoad
- Archive of Our Own
- FanFiction.net
- Wattpad
- ScribbleHub
- FreeWebNovel
- NovelFull
- LightNovelPub
- WebNovel
- NovelBin-style mirrors:
  - novelbin.com
  - novelbin.net
  - novelbin.me
  - novel-bin.com
  - novel-bin.net
  - novel-next.com
  - novelnext.com
- ReadNovelFull/readlightnovel-style mirrors:
  - readnovelfull.com
  - readnovelfull.me
  - readlightnovel.org
  - readlightnovel.me
  - readlightnovel.cc
  - readwn.com
  - novelonlinefree.com
- WordPress/blog-hosted translator sites:
  - wordpress.com subdomains
  - blogspot-style pages through the generic fallback
  - asianhobbyist.com
  - re-library.com
  - travistranslations.com
  - secondlifetranslations.com
  - lightnovelstranslations.com

For unsupported sites, Download Novel's Default Parser now auto-detects likely chapter links and common chapter content containers. You can still use Advanced Options to provide exact CSS selectors when needed.
